## Who Is Roronoa Zoro?

Zoro's goal is simple: become the world's greatest swordsman. His training is equally simple — and brutally hard. He lifts weights with his mouth. He does thousands of reps. He trains through injuries that would hospitalize normal people. His physique is one of the most iconic in anime: **massive, dense muscle with a shredded midsection and V-taper that looks carved from stone.**

This program channels Zoro's philosophy: **heavy compound lifting, high volume, and an unbreakable will.**

---

## Zoro's Physique Breakdown

- **Massive upper body** — broad shoulders, thick chest, powerful arms
- **Defined, strong core** — the foundation of every sword technique
- **Powerful legs** — often overlooked but critical for stance and power generation
- **Low body fat** — lean enough to show muscle detail year-round

**Estimated stats:** ~5'11", ~185 lbs, ~10% body fat

### Day 5 — Zoro's Special (High Volume)

This is Zoro's signature session — the one where he does 3,000 reps. We'll keep it sane but brutal:

**The Zoro 1,000 Rep Challenge:**

Complete all reps in as few sets as possible. Rest only when needed.

| Exercise | Total Reps |
|----------|-----------|
| Push-Ups | 200 |
| Bodyweight Squats | 200 |
| Sit-Ups | 200 |
| Pull-Ups | 100 |
| Dips | 100 |
| Lunges (each leg) | 100 |
| Burpees | 50 |
| Mountain Climbers (each) | 50 |

**Total: 1,000 reps.** Time yourself and try to beat it each week.

## Nutrition: Eating to Become the Greatest

Zoro needs to fuel both heavy strength work and high-volume endurance sessions.

### Caloric Target
Bodyweight (lbs) × 18 (muscle-building surplus)

### Macros
- **Protein:** 1g per lb
- **Carbs:** 2.5g per lb
- **Fats:** 0.4g per lb

### Zoro's Meal Blueprint

**Breakfast:** 5 eggs + 2 cups oatmeal + milk + banana

**Lunch:** 250g beef or chicken + 250g rice + vegetables

**Pre-Workout:** Sweet potato + protein shake

**Post-Workout:** White rice + tuna or chicken + fruit

**Dinner:** Steak + roasted vegetables + rice

**Before Bed:** Cottage cheese + nuts

## The Zoro Mindset

> *"I don't care what you do to me, but I won't let you hurt my friends. If you want to get through, you'll have to kill me first."*

Zoro's training rules:

1. **Never make excuses.** Zoro trains through everything. No "I'm tired" or "I'm busy."
2. **Sleep is sacred.** Zoro sleeps anywhere, anytime. 8+ hours minimum.
3. **Set one goal and pursue it absolutely.** Zoro has one dream. One. Focus yours.
4. **Embrace the grind.** The 1,000-rep days are what separate the greatest from the rest.
